Sailing Optimization

Sail calculates the minimal speed required,
to keep up with any change of heading.
Wind data should be Apparent¹

Boat Speed
Wind Speed
Wind Angle
Deviation

¹ wind(speed,angle) experienced when sailing, as affected by boat speed

$$ v_{min}=v_0 {\cos(\alpha) \over \cos(\alpha+\sigma)} $$ $$ \alpha := sin^{-1} { v_0 · sin(w_a) \over \sqrt{v_0^2+w_v^2-2v_0w_v\cos(w_a)}} $$

\(v_0\) initial velocity
\(\alpha\) true wind angle
\(w_\alpha\) apparent wind angle
\(w_v\) apparent wind velocity
\(\sigma\) change of heading

Fourier Transformation - DFT

The Discrete Fourier Transform (DFT) of a dataset D, is

$$ F(x) = a_0 + {2 \over N} \sum_{n=0}^{N \over 2} (K_{in}cos(\tau x)+K_{jn}sin(\tau x) ) $$

where \( a_0 \) is the mean of the data, and
the coefficients \(K_{in}\) and \(K_{jn}\) are defined as

$$ K_{in}=\sum_{i=0}^{N-1}D_ncos(\frac{\tau in}{N}) $$ $$ K_{jn}=\sum_{j=0}^{N-1}D_nsin(\frac{\tau in}{N}) $$
